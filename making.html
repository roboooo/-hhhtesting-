1st stage of making

in this 1st stage of making my 3D model I had to find the shape that I wanted to bulid my trike. I started off with a rectangle block that i decided to use as a leg.

2nd stage of making

In this stage I had to copy and paste the legs for my trike 4 times to get the right amount of legs. I then used the one of the legs and changed the width and height of it (see information page) to create the body.

3rd stage of making

In this stage of my model I had morphed two more of my pieces. The first peice I elongated into the tail for my trike. The second piece I turned into a cube to use as the base of the head for my model.

4th stage of making

In this stage I used and morphed four different blocks. The first block I morphed I turned into the fril at the back of the Triceratop's head. The second block I made smaller and thinner to put at the front of my model to make the snout. The third block I duplicated and turned them on an angle to make the back horns. The last block I used I just made shorter then the other two and made it the front horn.
